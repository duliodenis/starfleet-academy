# Starfleet Academy — Curriculum & Required Reading

A single-page interactive dashboard mapping a structured five-year Starfleet Academy curriculum to its canon-sourced required reading, including a graduate Master Year.

## What it is

The page is built around two questions:

1. **What would Starfleet cadets actually be required to read?** Across TOS, TNG, DS9, VOY, and ENT, characters explicitly cite or clearly imply specific texts — _The Art of War_, Garth of Izar's Axanar campaign studies, Judge Aaron Satie's judicial decisions, Zefram Cochrane's warp drive monographs, and more. These are collected and marked as either **Explicit** (a character says it was required reading) or **Implied** (it is clearly instructional material within the show).

2. **How would those readings fit into a coherent four-year — and graduate — curriculum?** The dashboard reconstructs a plausible Academy course schedule across five departments, organized by year and semester, with each course linked to its canon-sourced reading material.

## Departments

| Department | Color | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Command | Red | Leadership, tactics, strategy, command judgment |
| Engineering | Gold | Warp theory, propulsion, starship systems |
| Sciences | Blue | Scientific method, xenobiology, temporal mechanics |
| History & Law | Purple | Federation history, ethics, legal doctrine |
| Operations | Teal | Core protocols, deep space operations, fleet logistics |

## Curriculum structure

- **Years 1–4** — Core cadet program, two semesters per year. Courses are marked as required or **Elective** depending on track.
- **Year 5 (Master Year)** — Graduate-level seminars, one per department. Includes the Kobayashi Maru Command Simulation, Advanced Warp Field Research, Advanced Temporal Mechanics, Federation Charter & Treaty Law, and Strategic Fleet Operations.

## Filters

- **Curriculum / Required Reading** — switch between the course schedule and the reading list
- **Explicit Only** — show only readings with a direct on-screen citation
- **Series pills** — filter by TOS, TNG, DS9, VOY, or ENT (multi-select)
- **Year pills** — filter by academy year, including Master year (multi-select)
- **Dept pills** — filter courses by department (Curriculum mode only, multi-select)
- **Clear Filters** — reset all active filters

Selecting any row in either view opens a detail pane with the full course description, canon rationale, episode reference, and linked reading or courses.

## Canon scope

All explicit required-reading citations are preserved as stated in the shows. The four-year curriculum and the graduate year are structured reconstructions — built from those canon references plus the broader instructional material implied across the franchise.
